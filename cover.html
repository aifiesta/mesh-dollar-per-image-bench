The headline finding
A $0.008 model ties a $0.060 model on routine prompts.
On hyper-complex prompts the most expensive model drops to LAST place.
All 5 pilot models scored within 0.17 quality-points of each other on the first five categories — a 7.5× cost spread bought 3.4% of the 1–5 quality scale. On the hyper-complex (boss-fight) sub-pilot, the saturation collapses: GPT-Image-2 leads at Q=3.58 but takes 189 s per image. Imagen 4 Ultra drops to last.
